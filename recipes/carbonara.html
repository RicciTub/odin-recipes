Classic Carbonara!

Easy and quick way to make a tasty Pasta Carbonara

This authentic Italian Pasta Carbonara is a creamy, savory, and incredibly satisfying dish made with just a few simple ingredientsâ€”eggs, cheese, pancetta, and black pepper. The magic happens when the silky sauce coats the pasta without using cream, creating a rich and flavorful bite every time.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz (340g) spaghetti (or bucatini)
  • 4 oz (115g) pancetta (or guanciale), diced
  • 2 large eggs
  • 2 large egg yolks
  • 1 cup grated Pecorino Romano (or Parmesan)
  • 1 tsp freshly ground black pepper
  • Salt (for boiling water)

Instructions

  1. Cook the pasta in a large pot of boiling water until its al dente, reserve 1 cup of the pasta water.
  2. In a large pan cook the diced pancetta until its golden brown then remore from heat and let it sit while you prepare the sauce
  3. Afterwards prepare the sauce with the eggs, egg yolks, grated cheese and black pepper all whisked together in a bowl until smooth like a cream
  4. Add the drained pasta to the pan with the pancetta
  5. Remove from heat and add the creamy sauce, mixing until they are all even
  6. Add the cup of water to the pan and carefully until you get a sikly creamy sauce
  7. Serve immediatly!

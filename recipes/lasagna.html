Worlds best lasagna!

Learn how to prepare the best lasagna you will ever eat

This homemade lasagna is a rich and comforting dish layered with perfectly seasoned meat sauce, creamy ricotta, and gooey melted cheese. Each bite combines tender pasta, savory flavors, and a hint of herbs, making it a perfect meal for family dinners or special gatherings.

Ingredients

  • Minced Meat
  • Onion and Garlic
  • Tomato Products
  • Sugar
  • Seasonings
  • Noodles
  • Egg

Preparation Steps

  1. Make the meat sauce
  2. Cook the noodles
  3. make the mixture with riccota
  4. Add the layers in order
  5. Cover in foil before putting in the oven
  6. Serve

Order of the Layers

When prepare the dish its important to take care of how to add each layer in order for it end up with the perfect spread of ingredients.

  1. Meat Sauce
  2. Noodles
  3. Riccota Mixture
  4. Mozzarella Slices
  5. Meat Sauce
  6. Parmesan Cheese
  7. Repeat the layers, at the top add more Parmesan
